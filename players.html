Legendary Volleyball Players

Karch Kiraly

The only player to win Olympic gold in both indoor and beach volleyball. Won 3 Olympic gold medals (1984, 1988 indoor, 1996 beach).

Giba (Gilberto Amauri de Godoy Filho)

Brazilian legend, considered one of the best players of all time. Led Brazil to gold at the 2004 Olympics.

Lang Ping

Chinese volleyball icon known as the "Iron Hammer." Won Olympic gold as both player (1984) and coach (2016).
