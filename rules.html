Basic Volleyball Rules

Game Basics

  • Teams consist of 6 players on each side
  • Matches are best-of-five sets
  • First four sets go to 25 points (must win by 2)
  • Fifth set goes to 15 points (must win by 2)

Playing Rules

  • Each team gets up to three touches per possession
  • A player cannot hit the ball twice in succession
  • The ball must be hit, not caught or thrown
  • The ball must pass over the net between the antennas

Scoring

  • Rally scoring means a point is awarded on every serve
  • A point is scored when the ball lands in the opponent's court
  • A point is awarded if the opponent commits a fault
